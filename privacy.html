Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What is Easy Read?

Easy Read is a mobile app.

The app turns difficult texts into easy-to-read texts.

What does the app do with your photos?

The app uses your phone's camera.

You take a photo of a text.

The app reads the text from the photo.

Then it deletes the photo.

We do not save your photos.

Who do we share your data with?

We use a service called Anthropic.

Anthropic reads the text from your photo to adapt it.

We do not share your data with anyone else.

You can read Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy

Permissions the app needs

The app asks for permission to use your phone's camera.

This permission is needed for the app to work.

How to contact us

If you have questions, write to us.
