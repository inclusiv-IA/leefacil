How it works

Three steps. No fuss.

No technical knowledge needed.

1
📱

Hold your phone upright

For best results, hold your phone vertically. The camera captures the text better this way.

2
📷

Point and take a photo

Point the camera at the text you want to understand and take a photo. It can be a letter, a sign, a news article...

3
✅

Read in simple words

In seconds you get the adapted text, in short and easy-to-understand sentences.

🔒 Your photos are safe

  • 📷 You take a photo of the text.
  • 🚫 Your photos are not saved anywhere.
  • 👁️ Only you can see your photos.
  • 🤝 We do not share your photos with anyone.
  • 🗑️ After reading the text, the photo is gone.

Our story

From real life, for real life.

Easy Read was born from a real need.
I created this app to help my son face the adult world with greater independence.

Easy Read helps people understand difficult everyday texts: forms, contracts, instructions, important documents.

It is a tool for personal use. Its purpose is simple: to make comprehension easier and support a more independent life.

Especially useful for people with reading comprehension difficulties, intellectual disabilities, older adults, or anyone who needs to better understand a difficult text.
